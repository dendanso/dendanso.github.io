Meet the Team

CARPOOL GHANA

Commute and transport is everyone's business. From young people just starting out to professinals who have been in the business for years, our team is blessed with people who understand how important a hassle-free commute is to work productivity. We work to produce real, user-based soltion that meet transport needs.
Our most basic priniciple being innovtion because of our commitment to finding better ways to make transport easier; because everyone deserves good things and our service is for everyone-driver or rider; and community, because a true sense of achievment comes from that everyone helped and everyone is benefiting.


ABOUT Carpool GHANA

We are a transport service company leveraging on technology to provide high end transport solutions to commuters in Ghana through ridesharing.
